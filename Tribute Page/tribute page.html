Brief History

Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, English and Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture. It's motto is "Mes que un club"(More than a club) because the supporters own and operate the club. Making the club one of the most valuable sports teams in the world. It is worth over $3 billon. The only team that surprises it is Manchester United F.C.

It is one of the world's most popular futbol clubs in the world.
